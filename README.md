# US Accidents Data Analysis (EDA)
📌 Project Overview:

This project focuses on performing large-scale Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the US Accidents dataset containing 70+ million real-world accident records. The primary objective is to uncover meaningful insights, patterns, and risk factors influencing road accidents across the United States.

The analysis explores multiple dimensions including time-based trends, geographic distribution, weather and environmental conditions, and road infrastructure attributes. By processing and analyzing such a high-volume dataset, the project demonstrates the ability to handle large-scale data, perform deep analytical exploration, and generate data-driven insights using Python.

## Key areas explored in this project:

- Temporal accident patterns (hourly, daily, monthly, yearly).

- Geographic accident distribution across states and cities.

- Weather and visibility impact on accident occurrence and severity.

- Influence of road and traffic infrastructure (junctions, signals, crossings, etc.).

- Identification of high-risk locations and peak accident periods.

- Data cleaning, preprocessing, and large-scale dataset handling.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze accident trends across time, weather, and geography.

* Identify high-risk locations and peak accident periods.

* Understand factors influencing accident severity.

* Perform large-scale data cleaning and preprocessing.

* Generate clear and insightful visualizations.

* Discover patterns and risk factors from real-world data.

# 🔍 Key Work Performed
## Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

- Handled missing values and incomplete records.

- Treated outliers and validated data distributions.

- Removed inconsistencies and redundant entries.

- Prepared structured dataset for large-scale analysis.

## Exploratory Data Analysis

- Accident frequency by hour, day, month, and year.

- Weather and environmental impact on accident occurrence.

- Geographic accident distribution across states and cities.

- Severity and distance distribution analysis.

- Influence of road and traffic features on accident frequency.

## Visualization

- Time-series accident trends.

- Accident severity distribution.

- Weather vs accident pattern analysis.

- Geographic accident density.

- Feature relationship visualization.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Peak accident occurrence observed during high-traffic hours.

- Weather conditions and reduced visibility strongly influence accident severity.

- High accident concentration in dense urban and high-traffic regions.

- Road infrastructure factors (junctions, signals, crossings) contribute to accident frequency.

- Identified predictable temporal and environmental risk patterns.
